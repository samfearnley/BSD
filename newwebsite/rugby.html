Rules

The rules are if the ball goes out of the pitch you have to take a lineout which is when you put the ball over your head and spin it into the middle of both of the teams and both of the teams have to lift a player up and the players have to try and catch the ball and pass it out to the space. You are only allowed to use your feet if you are kicking the ball or taking a conversion. A conversion happens

after you score a trie so after thr trie you line the ball up to the same area as the ball hit when you scored the trie. Then you kick the ball of a stand and try and get it inbetween the 2 posts and if you do you score 2 points. You are not allowed to pass the ball forward or the other team gets a penalty. If you score a try you get 5 points. To score a try you have to run to the try line and place the ball on or behind the line if you dont it doesn't count.
